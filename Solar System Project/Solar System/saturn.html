SaturnTHE RINGED PLANET


Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and the second-largest in the Solar System, after Jupiter. It is a gas giant with an average radius of about nine-and-a-half times that of Earth. It has only one-eighth the average density of Earth, but is over 95 times more massive.Saturn has the most extensive ring system in the Solar System. Its rings are made up of rock, dust and icy material.
