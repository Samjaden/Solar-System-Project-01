SUN


The Sun is the star at the center of the Solar System. It is a massive, hot ball of plasma, inflated and heated by energy produced by nuclear fusion reactions at its core

Planet Type Star
Moons None
Age 4.603 billion years
Mass 1.989 × 10^30 kg
Gravity 274 m/s²
Radius 696,340 km
Average Temperature 5,600 °C
Composition Primarily hydrogen and helium
Rotation Period Approximately 25 days at the equator, 35 days at the poles
